## What Data We Process

This website is a static site and does not set tracking cookies. When you contact
us by email, we process the data you provide (name, email address, message) solely
to respond to your request.

When the site is accessed, the hosting provider may temporarily store standard
server log data (IP address, date and time of access, requested page, browser type)
for the purpose of operating and securing the website.

## External Content

Some pages embed or link to external services (e.g. map data from OpenStreetMap
and fonts served by Google Fonts). When such content is loaded, the respective
provider may receive technical data such as your IP address.

## Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, and erase your personal data, to restrict or
object to its processing, and to data portability. You may also lodge a complaint
with a supervisory authority.
